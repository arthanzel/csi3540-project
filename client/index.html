Quantum of Cells

Rapid prototyping of dynamical systems

Solving differential equations no longer requires buying and learning Matlab. Quantum of Cells is an application that lets you quickly design and troubleshoot dynamical systems right in your browser.

Whether you make dynamical systems in a lab, in class, or simply for fun, Quantum of Cells is the fastest way to create and collaborate on dynamical models with your colleagues.

Image

Not Only Cells…

Quantum of Cells was developed at the University of Ottawa to simulate genetic networks in living cells. Since then, it has grown into a practical tool for mocking up many other dynamical systems.

Quantum of Cells is free, and always will be.
